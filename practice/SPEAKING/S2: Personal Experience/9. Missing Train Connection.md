# Question

Talk about your experience missing a train connection. Maybe you can talk about how you managed the situation, alternative arrangements you made, or what you learned from the experience. What happened, how did you handle it, and what impact did this experience have on you?

Recording time: 60s

# Answer

Considering my personal history, missing a crucial train connection during a busy trip to Tokyo stands out as truly extraordinary.
Primarily, the aspect of how my initial train was delayed by twenty minutes is what makes this memory so vivid. Given that I only had a five-minute window to transfer, I realized I would miss the last train of the night, which was a formidable challenge. I was initially filled with a sense of dread and utter confusion.
Concerning how I handled the situation, I had to swiftly track down a station employee to sort out the details. Since no more trains were running, I had to bite the bullet and pay for a pricey taxi to reach my hotel. This experience taught me to always think ahead and stay on the ball when planning complex trips.
Undoubtedly, this experience remains a magnificent part of my past.
