# Question

Talk about an unusual weather event you experienced. Maybe it was a spectacular thunderstorm, an unexpected snowfall in summer, or a remarkable rainbow. What was the weather phenomenon, how did people around you react, and why did this experience leave a lasting impression on you?

Recording time: 60s

# Answer

Considering my personal history, witnessing a sudden, spectacular thunderstorm during a summer beach trip stands out as truly extraordinary.

Primarily, the aspect of how the bright sky turned abruptly murky is what makes this memory so vivid.

Everyone had been lucking out with the sun earlier, but we suddenly had to watch out for immense lightning bolts. People around me were absolutely shocked; they began to head off and run into nearby buildings to find cover swiftly.

Concerning my emotions at that time, I was initially filled with dread, but watching the raw power of nature was also quite intriguing. Once the weather started to clear up, a magnificent rainbow appeared over the ocean, which provided a huge sense of relief.

Undoubtedly, this experience remains a magnificent part of my past.
