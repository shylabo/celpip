# Question

Talk about your first experience traveling alone. Maybe you went on a business trip, took a vacation, or visited family in another city by yourself. Where did you go, what challenges did you face, and what made this journey particularly memorable for you?

Recording time: 60s

# Answer

Considering my personal history, my first solo vacation to New York City stands out as truly extraordinary.

Primarily, the aspect of navigating the complex subway system alone is what makes this memory so vivid.

Initially, it was a formidable challenge, and I was filled with dread about getting lost in such an immense city. I had to bite the bullet and figure out the routes while staying on the ball to avoid missing my stops.

Concerning my emotions during the journey, I actually lucked out when a friendly local helped me find the right platform. Once I finally reached Times Square, the radiant lights were astounding, and I felt a huge sense of jubilation and independence.

Undoubtedly, this experience remains a magnificent part of my past.
