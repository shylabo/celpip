# Question

Describe an experience where you helped support a friend through a difficult situation. Maybe you assisted them during a personal crisis, offered emotional support during a challenging time, or stood by them when they needed encouragement. What was the situation, how did you provide support, and what impact did this experience have on your friendship?

Recording time: 60s

# Answer

Considering my personal history, helping a close friend navigate a sudden job loss stands out as truly extraordinary.
Primarily, the aspect of his unexpected unemployment was a major struggle that left him feeling quite terrified about his future. I chose to offer my full support by helping him brush up on his interview skills and reviewing his applications daily to ensure they were perfect. Provided that we practiced every evening, his confidence began to return swiftly.
Though the situation was initially filled with dread, seeing him eventually land a new position was a moment of pure jubilation. This shared experience had a positive impact on our friendship, making our bond much more sincere and amicable.
Undoubtedly, this experience remains a magnificent part of my past.
