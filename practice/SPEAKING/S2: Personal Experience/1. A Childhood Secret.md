# Question

Tell me about a secret you kept during your childhood. Maybe it was a hidden treasure, a surprise for someone special, or something funny you didn't want others to know about. What was the secret, how long did you keep it, and how did you feel about having this secret?

Recording time: 60s

# Answer

Considering my personal history, keeping a stray kitten a secret from my parents stands out as truly extraordinary.

Primarily, the aspect of hiding this delicate creature in our backyard shed for two weeks is what makes this memory so vivid.

Every night, I had to sneak out of my room to feed it, and I even managed to smuggle some milk from the kitchen without my mom catching on.

Concerning my emotions at that time, I was initially filled with dread about getting caught, but caring for that tiny cat brought me such jubilation and delight.

Keeping the secret felt like a piece of cake until my sister finally spilled the beans to my parents.

Undoubtedly, this experience remains a magnificent part of my past.
