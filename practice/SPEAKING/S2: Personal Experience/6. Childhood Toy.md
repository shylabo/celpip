# Question

Talk about your favorite toy from childhood. Maybe it was a special gift, something you saved up to buy, or a handmade item. What was the toy, how did you get it, and why was it particularly special to you?

Recording time: 60s

# Answer

Considering my personal history, my favorite childhood toy was a robust remote-controlled car that I bought with my own savings.
Primarily, the aspect of how I managed to get it is what makes the memory so vivid. I had to cut back on buying snacks and toys for nearly a year just to afford it. Provided that I stayed on the ball with my small allowance, finally being able to purchase it felt like a piece of cake.
Concerning why it was so special to me, it provided a superb way to kick back and blow off steam after a rigorous day at school. My father and I would often head off to the park to try out different stunts and races. Although I was initially filled with dread about potentially wrecking the car, those afternoons were filled with jubilation.
Undoubtedly, this experience remains a magnificent part of my past.
