# Question

Tell me about your first experience buying something online. Maybe it was clothing, electronics, or something unique you couldn't find locally. What did you buy, how did you feel about the process, and what made this shopping experience memorable?

Recording time: 60s

# Answer

Considering my personal history, purchasing an antique watch online for the first time stands out as truly extraordinary.
Primarily, the aspect of navigating the complex website to complete the transaction is what makes this memory so vivid. I remember having to sign in and carefully look over the seller's reputation to ensure the product was authentic.
Concerning my emotions at that time, I was initially filled with dread that the package might be a rip off or arrive in substandard condition. However, after it reached my house swiftly, I felt a massive sense of jubilation and relief because the watch was superb.
Undoubtedly, this experience remains a magnificent part of my past.
