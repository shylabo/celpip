# Question

Talk about a memorable office celebration you attended. Maybe it was a retirement party, a milestone achievement, or a festive holiday gathering. What was the occasion, how did everyone participate, and what made this celebration particularly special for you and your colleagues?

Recording time: 60s

# Answer

Considering my personal history, a festive gathering we had at the office to celebrate a major project milestone stands out as truly extraordinary.
Primarily, the aspect of how every single colleague participated is what makes this memory so vivid. We all decided to dress up in formal attire and arranged a massive batch of local delicacies to share in the breakroom. Given that the event included a surprise gift exchange, seeing the delight and jubilation on everyone’s faces was astounding.
Concerning why this celebration was particularly special, it provided a superb opportunity for our team to kick back and blow off steam after a rigorous month of work. Since we had all been in the same boat regarding our tight deadlines, finally reaching our goal culminated in a huge sense of relief.
Undoubtedly, this experience remains a magnificent part of my past.
