# Question

You live in an apartment building where residents share a laundry room. The current policy allows each resident to use the machines for up to 2 hours per week during their assigned time slot.

Your elderly neighbor, Mrs. Chen, has requested to switch her laundry time slot with yours. She explains that her current slot (8 PM) is too late for her, and your slot (2 PM) would be much better for her schedule.

However, 2 PM is the only time that works for you due to your work schedule, and changing it would mean you'd have to do laundry at midnight.

Choose ONE:
EITHER
Talk to Mrs. Chen. Explain why you cannot switch laundry time slots with her.
OR
Talk to the building manager. Explain why you think Mrs. Chen should be given a different resident's daytime slot.

Recording time: 60s
