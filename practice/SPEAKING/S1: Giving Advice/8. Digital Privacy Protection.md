# Question

Your friend is concerned about their online privacy and security after hearing about recent data breaches in the news. They want to better protect their personal information online but don't know where to start. Give them advice on what steps they should take to enhance their digital privacy and security.

Recording time: 90s

# Answer

Hi John, how have you been? It feels like ages since we last caught up! I heard about the formidable challenges you're currently facing regarding your online privacy, and I wanted to share some insights based on my own experiences.
First and foremost, you should beef up your digital defenses by using a password manager. Given that recent data breaches have become an abrupt and serious threat, having unique, complex passwords for every site is vital. This rigorous approach will help you attain better security swiftly.
Furthermore, it is crucial to look into enabling two-factor authentication for your most sensitive accounts. Provided that you stay on the ball and remember to sign out of your accounts on shared devices, keeping your personal information secure will eventually feel like a piece of cake. You must look out for any suspicious or vague emails that might aim at stealing your data.
I sincerely hope you find these recommendations helpful. Please don’t let the news cause you too much dread; you can definitely pull off a more secure digital life! Take care!
