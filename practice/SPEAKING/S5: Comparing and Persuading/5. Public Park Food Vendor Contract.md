# Question

You are responsible for selecting a food vendor for the city's most popular public park. You need to decide which type of food vendor would be best for park visitors. You find two suitable options. Choose the option that you prefer. In the next section, you will need to persuade the park manager that your choice is the better choice.

1. Mobile Food Cart
   • requires minimal space
   • serves quick snacks and beverages
   • $500 monthly rent
   • operates 7 days a week, 9am-6pm
   • easy to relocate based on crowd flow

2. Small Permanent Café
   • built-in seating area for 20 people
   • serves hot meals and beverages
   • $1200 monthly rent
   • operates 7 days a week, 8am-8pm
   • includes public restroom maintenance

The park manager is suggesting another food vendor option. Persuade the park manager that the option you chose is more suitable by comparing the two.

Park Manager's Choice: Weekend Food Truck Rotation
• different food trucks each weekend
• varied cuisine options
• $300 per weekend per truck
• operates weekends only, 11am-7pm
• no permanent infrastructure needed

Recording time: 60s

# Answer

Hi [Manager's Name], glad to talk to you again! Deciding between these food vendor plans is certainly a tough choice; nevertheless, it’s clear that the Small Permanent Café provides a more advantageous option for our park visitors.

First and foremost, the café’s schedule is vital. Since it operates seven days a week from 8am to 8pm, we can provide consistent service, whereas your food truck suggestion only covers weekends [Source]. Given that this is the city's most popular park, visitors who arrive on weekdays shouldn't have to hold off on eating because no vendors are available. This rigorous schedule ensures we meet the public’s needs swiftly.

Furthermore, although the café is more pricey at $1200 a month, it includes public restroom maintenance and built-in seating for twenty people [96, Source]. These amenities are integral to a superb visitor experience. Providing a permanent place for families to kick back and relax ensures the park doesn't become a scene of utter confusion. Provided that we maintain these high standards, our park will continue to prosper.
